About Me

Prof. A.N. Vijayakumar is a Professor of Finance and Entrepreneurship at the Central University of Karnataka, specializing in financial markets, entrepreneurship, and strategic management. His research interests include derivatives, investment management, sustainable finance, augmenting farmers’ income, financial inclusion, and Indian management ethos.


Over the years, Prof. Vijayakumar has been associated with and has served at several reputed institutions, including the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (New Delhi), the Indian Institute of Plantation Management (Bengaluru), the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SRO Chennai), and the Multi Commodity Exchange of India Limited (Mumbai).


As an academician, he has taught at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels and has organized numerous management and faculty development programs. He has also undertaken research and consultancy projects with the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India, as well as with the Coffee Board, Rubber Board, Spices Board, Tea Board, and the Karnataka State Agricultural Prices Commission, Government of Karnataka. Additionally, he has collaborated with organizations such as the ICSSR, ICAR, and others.
