About Me

I am a Professor of Finance and Entrepreneurship at the Central University of Karnataka, specializing in financial markets including equities, commodities, and currency exchanges. My academic and research interests include derivatives, price risk management, option valuation, and sustainable finance.


My research primarily focuses on agricultural finance, commodity markets, and Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) in India, aiming to strengthen rural and institutional financial ecosystems. I have also contributed to the study of Indian management ethos, corporate governance, and entrepreneurial development.


Over the years, I have been associated with several reputed academic institutions and have received recognition for my research contributions in commodity markets, derivatives, and financial risk management.
