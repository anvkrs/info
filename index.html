About Me

I am a Professor of Finance & Entrepreneurship at Central University of Karnataka, specializing in price risk, derivatives, and investment management. My research focuses on agricultural finance, commodity markets, and farmer producer organisations in India. Previously, I have worked with various academic institutions, receiving recognition for my research in commodity markets and corporate governance.

Research Interests

  • Price Risk & Derivatives: Research on hedging and futures contracts.
  • Green Finance: Sustainable finance for rural development.
  • Investment Management: Efficiency of capital and portfolio management.
  • Entrepreneurship: Empowering small and marginal farmers through FPOs.
