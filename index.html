About Me

I am a Professor of Finance and Entrepreneurship at the Central University of Karnataka, specializing in financial markets, entrepreneurship, and strategic management. My research interests include derivatives, investment management, sustainable finance, augmenting farmers’ income, financial inclusion, and Indian management ethos.


Over the years, I have been associated with and have served at reputed institutions such as the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (New Delhi), Indian Institute of Plantation Management (Bengaluru), Securities and Exchange Board of India (SRO Chennai), and the Multi Commodity Exchange of India Limited (Mumbai).


During my tenure as an academician, in addition to teaching at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels, I have also organized management and faculty development programs. I have worked on research and consultancy projects with the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India, as well as with the Coffee Board, Rubber Board, Spices Board, Tea Board, and the Karnataka State Agricultural Prices Commission, Government of Karnataka, in addition to organizations such as the ICSSR, ICAR, and others.
